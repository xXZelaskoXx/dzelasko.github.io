Our Story

It all started in 1957 when Doug Sr. started his boiling water cart. It would be another ten years before he had the idea to put things inside the boiled water to see if they sold any better.

Doug Sr.'s Boiled Water Cart, taken as close to the city of Witchita as he was legally permitted to be.

"Doug's Dogs is registered as a restaurant with the county."

The business has been handed down over generations. From Doug Sr. all the way down to Doug III, who now runs the business.

Doug III getting hot dogs at a competitor's stand. "My doctor told me to stop eating our food."

"Every dog is made with ingredients!"

Do you like your dogs cold? Do you like your dogs lukewarm? However you like your dogs, Doug's Dogs has the dogs for you.
